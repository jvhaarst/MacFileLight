Version 0.2 - 2006-10-03

* Scanning
	- Directories are scanned three times faster.
	- A progress bar is shown while scanning.

* Drawing
	- The sizes of files and directories are now displayed.
	- Prettier, lower-contrast colours are used.
	- Files are distinguished from directories by displaying in grayscale.
	- When the cursor is over a clickable object, it changes to a hand.

* Interaction
	- When a directory is clicked on, the view zooms in.
	- Right-clicking gives a context-menu, where you can:
		+ Open files
		+ Reveal files in the Finder
		+ Trash files
		+ Copy a file's path to the clipboard
	- A toolbar lets you view the parent directory, or refresh the view.
